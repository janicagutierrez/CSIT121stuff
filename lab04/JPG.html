What is the love triange trope?

The imfamous trope is set between three characters which centers around the main central character as it involves drama, jealously, romance, sadness, and conflict within a storyline. This helps within the storyline of a book as it can explore various of character developments and plot lines which can be significant into storytelling by showcasing the character's feelings, thoughts, and actions between one another.
