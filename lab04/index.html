Love Tropes in Romance Books

What are Love Tropes?

The term "love tropes" explains on the two main characters dynamic throughout their story in the book. Love tropes are storytelling techniques to help the reader recognize the familiar connection between the two characters. This specific technique can can identify through scenarious within books as it can further adapt the character's story arc with one another and in the plot.
