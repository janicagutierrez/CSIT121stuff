About this Image

In this GIF, it shows one of the ending scenes from the infamous movie "The Breakfast Club". Within the movie, it shows the classic trope of opposites attract as both couples are paired up with people that are completely opposite of them such as how popular they are in high school, their looks, the way they act or dress, and their friends. In this one scene, it is two people who are completely opposite from one another as the girl is a loner within the movie and the boy is a high school athlete in the wrestling team.

What is the opposites attract trope?

This trope can help be described and viewed as two people who come from different backgrounds such as friend, looks, personalities, personal lifes, and beleifs who still find a romantic connection with one another depsite the circumstances. Readers of books are fond to this trope as it brings excitement and yearning in a book as this can be further adapted through personal scenes between the characters and the growth of the blossoming relationship. This brings drama, tension, and character developments within a story as both characters understand and try to see the other in a different perspective.
