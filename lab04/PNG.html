This is the book cover of "The Risk" by Elle Kennedy which involves the enemies to lovers love trope. This type of cover is widely used throughout romance and the young adult book community as it can show illustrations of the main characters of the cover without having the need to hire real people for said cover. This specific book cover shows the two main characters within the book as it is in a college hockey team setting involving one text that can lure readers in.

What is the enemies to lovers trope?

This is also one of an infamous love tropes the book community uses within a storyline and plotline. This involves two characters who strongly dislike one another as it involves conflict, circumstances, personal feelings, emotional and sexual tension, and character development. It is fairly popular and common to find this said trope within romantic novels as it hooks readers on through high tensions between both characters as it can express passionate obstacles and developments.
